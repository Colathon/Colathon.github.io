# Welcome to my Personal Wiki

This is where I store my fragmented thoughts, structured knowledge, and quick notes. Unlike the blog, which focuses on long-form articles, this wiki is a "Digital Garden"—a place where ideas are planted and allowed to grow over time.

## How it works

- **Non-linear:** Entries are interconnected via internal links.
- **Evolving:** Pages are updated as I learn more about a topic.
- **AI-Managed:** My AI Agent helps me maintain the structure and cross-references here.
